1. Privacy Summary

✓ The Short Version

PNG·PDF Bridge is built with privacy as its foundation. Unlike other online converters that upload your files to servers, everything happens directly in your browser. Your images never leave your device. We don't have servers, don't collect data, and can't see your files even if we wanted to.

3. File Handling

🔒 100% Client-Side Processing: When you upload a PNG file, it is read by your browser using the FileReader API. The file data is stored temporarily in your device's memory (RAM) and is never transmitted over the internet.

🖼️ Thumbnail Generation: Thumbnails are created using the Canvas API, again entirely on your device.

📄 PDF Creation: PDF generation happens using PDF-Lib, a JavaScript library that runs locally. The resulting PDF is created in your browser's memory.

🗑️ Automatic Cleanup: Once you close the tab or browser, all file data is automatically cleared from memory. No traces remain.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We do not use any tracking cookies, analytics tools, or fingerprinting techniques.

🍪 Local Storage Only: The only data stored on your device is your theme preference (light/dark mode) in your browser's local storage. This is not a cookie and cannot be used to track you.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party libraries, all loaded via CDN:

  • Tailwind CSS - For styling (loaded from CDN)
  • Google Fonts (Inter) - For typography
  • PDF-Lib - For PDF generation

These libraries are loaded in your browser and do not send your data to their servers. Your files are never exposed to these services.

6. Data Retention

Files stay in memory only while the page is open.

After Closing:

All data is immediately cleared from memory.

No Servers:

7. Security Measures

  • HTTPS Only: Our site is served over HTTPS, ensuring encrypted connection.
  • No Data Transmission: Your files never leave your device.
  • Memory Safety: File data is stored in JavaScript variables and garbage-collected when done.
  • No Third-Ptery Access: We don't share anything because we have nothing to share.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is safe for users of all ages because no data is collected. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU). Since we don't collect anything, there's no risk of child data being stored.
